# Traffic-Analytics-Dashboard
1.Project Title

Bengaluru Traffic Analytics Dashboard

2.Short Description / Purpose

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes traffic congestion, volume, speed, and travel time across major Bengaluru areas and roads. The project uncovers how factors like weather, roadworks, parking usage, signals, and public transport impact congestion—supporting data-driven traffic management and smart city planning.

3.Tech Stack
📊 Microsoft Power BI Desktop
🧮 DAX (measures & calculated columns)
🗂️ Data Modeling & Relationships
🎛️ Interactive visuals, slicers, KPI cards
🧹 Data cleaning & transformation with Power Query

4.Data Source
Dataset sourced from Kaggle (Bengaluru traffic dataset)

5.Features / Highlights
a.Area-wise traffic and congestion comparison across Electronic City, Hebbal, Indiranagar, Jayanagar, Koramangala, M. G. Road, Whitefield, and Yeshwanthpur
b.Road and intersection performance analysis including Silk Board Junction, Hebbal Flyover, Sarjapur Road, Trinity Circle, and Sony World Junction
c.Weather conditions impact on traffic volume and travel time
d.Effect of road construction on congestion levels
e.Relationship between parking usage and traffic density
f.Public transport usage vs congestion comparison
g.Traffic signal status influence on traffic flow
h.Monthly traffic volume trend analysis
i.Interactive filters and slicers for detailed analysis

6.Key Questions Addressed
Which areas in Bengaluru experience the highest traffic congestion and volume?
Which roads and intersections are the most traffic-prone?
How do weather conditions affect traffic volume and travel time?
What is the impact of road construction activities on congestion levels?
Does higher parking usage contribute to increased traffic density?
How does public transport usage influence congestion?
What role do traffic signals play in traffic distribution?
How does traffic volume vary month by month?

7.Business Insights
Areas like Koramangala and M.G. Road show consistently higher traffic volume compared to other regions.
Roads such as Hebbal Flyover, Silk Board Junction, and Sarjapur Road are major congestion hotspots.
Rainy and foggy weather conditions significantly increase congestion and travel time.
Road construction activities directly lead to spikes in congestion levels.
Increased parking usage is associated with higher traffic density in busy areas.
Locations with better public transport usage show relatively controlled congestion.
Traffic signal status has a measurable effect on smooth traffic flow.
Monthly trend analysis helps identify peak traffic periods and seasonal patterns.
